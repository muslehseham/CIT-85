Which means that we never seem to have the ingredients, nor the tools, that our recipies require - so we always have to go shopping in order to stock up our necessities to get our cravings satisfied. Thus the name 'The Unprepared Chef,' was born.

MILLE CREPE CAKE

Ingredients

CREPES
6 tablespoons butter
3 cups milk
6 eggs
1 ½ cups flour
⅔ cup cocoa powder
7 tablespoons sugar
4 cups whipped cream

GANACHE
1 cup heavy cream
4 tablespoons sugar
6 oz chocolate
1 tablespoon butter
¼ cup powdered sugar, optional for topping

Preparation

Make the crepes by melting the butter and allowing it to brown slightly. Set aside.
In the same pot, warm the milk. Remove from heat.
In a bowl, whisk flour, cocoa powder, and sugar. Mix in eggs.
Gradually mix in butter and warm milk. Chill.
On a nonstick skillet over medium heat, pour ¼ cup (60 ml) of crepe batter, and tip the pan to cover the entire bottom surface. Cook until bottom surface of crepe begins to brown, then flip.
Cool crepe. Repeat until all of the crepe batter is used.
Stack crepes on top of each other, with whipped cream between each layer. Once assembled, trim edges for a cleaner look, if desired.
For the ganache, bring the heavy cream and sugar to a boil, and pour over chocolate and butter. Let cool.
Spread over the crepe cake.
Top with sifted powdered sugar (optional).
Enjoy!

BISCOFF LOTUS NO-BAKE CAKE

Ingredients

1 jar of Biscoff Lotus Cookie Butter
2 packets of Biscoff Lotus cookies
2 12oz tubs of cool whip 2 tbsp Nescafe with 2 cups of hot water

Preparation

Dip the lotus cookie into the Nescafe then place it in a 9 by 13 pan. Keep repeating this step until there is a flat layer of dipped lotus cookies.
Top that off with the cool whip and spread evenly.
Add another layer of the Nescafe dipped lotus cookies. Then top off with another layer of the cream.
Smooth this very well.
Place in the freezer for 30 minutes.
Once out of the freezer, melt half a jar of biscoff in a microwave-safe bowl. Pour on top of the lotus cake and spread it out quickly before it cools and evenly.
Crumble a few lotus cookies and scatter the crumbs around the edges and corners of the pan.
Enjoy!

OREO TRUFFLES

Ingredients

36 chocolate sandwich cookies
8 oz cream cheese, softened
12 oz white chocolate, melted

Preparation

In a food processor, finely crush the cookies, reserving about 2 tablespoons of the mixture for sprinkling on top of the truffles.
In a large bowl, combine the cookie crumbs and cream cheese, stirring until evenly mixed. Chill the mixture for about an hour or until it can be rolled into a ball and hold its shape.
Divide and roll the mixture into golf ball-sized balls. Dip the truffles in the melted white chocolate and place on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Sprinkle the reserved cookie crumbs on top of the truffles before the chocolate hardens.
Enjoy!
